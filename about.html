About

Gear, process, and why I shoot.

I shoot the parts of a city most people walk past — stairwells, crosswalks, overcast skylines. Photography has always pulled at me — a way to see things a little differently. That pull grew into a real passion: exploring new places, new subjects, always chasing a fresh angle. Every shoot teaches me something I carry into the next. My kit has grown lens by lens, and each one has sharpened how I see.

I look at ordinary places and try to show them differently — even a street someone's walked a hundred times. Moments move fast, so I stay ready; a missed shot rarely comes back. When I can, I walk a location first and let a plan form before I shoot. Everyone sees the world a little differently — mine just comes through a camera.

Based in Moncton, working across the Maritimes.
